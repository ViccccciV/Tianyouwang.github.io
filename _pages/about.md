I am a second-year Master's student in Robotics at the [General Robotics, Automation, Sensing & Perception (GRASP) Laboratory](https://www.grasp.upenn.edu/) at the University of Pennsylvania, advised by [Prof.&nbsp;Dinesh&nbsp;Jayaraman](https://www.seas.upenn.edu/~dineshj/) and [Prof.&nbsp;Kostas&nbsp;Daniilidis](https://www.cis.upenn.edu/~kostas/). 

My research interests lie at the intersection of robot learning and 3D computer vision, with a focus on enabling robots to learn from humans and adapt to diverse,&nbsp;unstructured.&nbsp;environments.
